My Journey in Motion & Storytelling

My journey into visual storytelling began long before formal training. Prior to dedicating a year to intensive videography and photography studies, I gained invaluable experience in diverse professional environments, cultivating a strong work ethic and a versatile skill set. Upon completing my specialized education, I immediately sought hands-on industry experience. My initial four-month mission with a dynamic company was pivotal, allowing me to deeply understand professional workflows, adapt to fast-paced production cycles, and seamlessly integrate into collaborative team environments.

I trained and studied at TOM Training Center for 1 year, a period that significantly enhanced my skills and refined my creative approach. This foundational period solidified my commitment to crafting impactful visual narratives and prepared me for the exciting challenges of the creative industry.
